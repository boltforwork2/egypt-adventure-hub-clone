A city sightseeing tour around Hurghada that allows visitors to explore the local lifestyle and main attractions.

### What's Included
1. El Mina Mosque
2. Hurghada Marina
3. Local bazaars
4. Walking through Hurghada streets

### Additional Details for Tour Detail Page

The tour includes walking through authentic Hurghada streets, away from the tourist areas, allowing guests to experience genuine Egyptian culture and hospitality.

## 2. Grand Aquarium – Hurghada

### Basic Information
- **Tour Name:** Grand Aquarium – Hurghada
- **Tour ID:** `grand-aquarium-hurghada`
- **Category:** Leisure
- **Duration:** Flexible Duration
- **Location:** Hurghada, Egypt
- **Location Badge:** Hurghada Trip

### Full Description
The Aquarium Tour is one of the best family-friendly entertainment experiences in Hurghada, combining marine life observation with educational and interactive attractions.

### Key Highlights
- More than 1,200 marine animals
- Around 100 different species
- Nearly 1 million gallons of water
- 24 different marine life exhibitions
- Underwater tunnel walking experience

### What's Included
1. More than 1,200 marine animals representing around 100 species
2. Nearly 1 million gallons of water
3. 24 different marine life exhibitions
4. Underwater tunnel walking experience
5. Aquarium entry
6. Rainforest area
7. Small zoo

### Opening Hours
- **Daily Operating Hours:** 9:00 AM to 1:00 AM
- **Last Entry:** Usually at 11:00 PM
- **Shark Feeding Shows:**
  - First Show: 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  - Second Show: 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM

### Additional Details for Tour Detail Page
The Grand Aquarium in Hurghada is a world-class marine facility that houses an impressive collection of Red Sea marine life and species from around the globe. Visitors can walk through a spectacular underwater tunnel, offering 360-degree views of sharks, rays, and colorful tropical fish swimming overhead and around them. The facility includes themed zones representing different marine ecosystems, interactive touch pools, and educational displays about marine conservation. Beyond the aquarium itself, the ticket includes access to a recreated rainforest environment complete with exotic birds and reptiles, as well as a small zoo area. This makes it an ideal destination for families with children, offering both entertainment and educational value.

## 3. Horse Riding Tour – Hurghada

### Basic Information
- **Tour Name:** Horse Riding Tour – Hurghada
- **Tour ID:** `horse-riding-hurghada`
- **Category:** Leisure
- **Duration:** 1–2 Hours
- **Location:** Hurghada, Egypt
- **Location Badge:** Hurghada Trip

### Full Description
Horse riding tours in Hurghada are suitable for all ages and offer a unique mix of natural scenery and enjoyable physical activity.

### Tour Overview
- Tours take place along sandy beaches or nearby desert areas
- Suitable for beginners and experienced riders
- Professional trainers provided
- Includes basic horse riding training and guidance
- Riding pace adjusted to participant skill level (walking or running)

### What's Included
1. Tours along sandy beaches or nearby desert areas
2. Suitable for beginners and experienced riders
3. Professional trainers provided
4. Basic horse riding training and guidance
5. Riding pace adjusted to participant skill level

### Available Times
- **Morning Rides:** 7:00 AM – 11:00 AM
  - Best for: Sunrise experience and cooler temperatures

- **Evening Rides:** 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM
  - Best for: Sunset experience and romantic atmosphere

### Additional Details for Tour Detail Page
Experience the beauty of Hurghada's coastline or desert landscape from horseback. This tour caters to all experience levels, from complete beginners who have never ridden before to seasoned equestrians. Professional trainers begin with a safety briefing and basic riding instructions for novices, ensuring everyone feels comfortable before setting off. Riders can choose between a peaceful beach route where horses walk along the water's edge with waves lapping at their hooves, or a desert trail that winds through sandy dunes and offers panoramic views of the Red Sea.

The horses are well-trained, gentle, and carefully matched to each rider's skill level. Beginners typically maintain a walking pace with close trainer supervision, while more experienced riders may opt for a faster trot or canter. Morning tours offer the magical experience of riding during sunrise, when the light is soft and temperatures are coolest. Evening tours provide stunning sunset views, creating perfect photo opportunities and a romantic atmosphere. All necessary equipment is provided, and no previous riding experience is required.
